Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026 | Effective Date: June 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BeastsNetwork ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at https://beastsnetwork.win (the "Site") and engage with the BeastsNetwork cryptocurrency token ("BeastsNetwork Token" or "Token").

By accessing or using our Site and interacting with the BeastsNetwork Token, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Site or engage with our Token.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, improve, and protect our services related to the BeastsNetwork Token.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Contact & Inquiry Information:

  • Name and email address when you contact us
  • Wallet address (if you voluntarily provide it for support)
  • Any other information you choose to share in communications

Newsletter & Updates:

  • Email address for receiving project updates
  • Preferences for communication

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our Site
  • Referring page information

Technical Data:

  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Session data

2.3 Blockchain Data

Please note that when you interact with the BeastsNetwork Token on the blockchain, certain information is publicly recorded and visible on the blockchain, including:

  • Your public wallet address
  • Transaction amounts and timestamps
  • Transaction hashes
  • Token balances

We do not control the blockchain and cannot modify or delete information recorded on it.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes related to the BeastsNetwork Token project:

3.1 Project Operations

  • To provide information about the BeastsNetwork Token
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To send project updates and announcements
  • To maintain and improve our Site

3.2 Communication

  • To send you newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • To provide important security notifications
  • To respond to your questions about the Token

3.3 Analytics & Improvement

  • To analyze usage patterns of our Site
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To develop new features for the BeastsNetwork ecosystem

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

  • When you explicitly authorize us to share your information

4.2 For Legal Compliance

  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • To respond to law enforcement requests
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others

4.3 Service Providers

  • With third-party service providers who assist us in operating our Site and services
  • These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us

4.4 Business Transfers

In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company.

5. Your Choices and Rights

5.1 Communication Preferences

You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications. Even if you opt-out, we may still send you non-promotional communications, such as security notifications or transaction confirmations.

5.2 Access and Control

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information that we hold. Please contact us using the information below to make such requests.

5.3 Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our Site. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Blockchain transactions are inherently public and immutable. Once a transaction is recorded on the blockchain, it cannot be altered or deleted.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Blockchain data is retained permanently as it is part of the public ledger.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites, services, or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third party before providing them with your personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site and the BeastsNetwork Token are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information about your child, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users

If you are accessing our Site from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using our Site, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
