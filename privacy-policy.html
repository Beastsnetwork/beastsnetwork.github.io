Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026 | Effective Date: June 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BeastsNetwork ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at https://beastsnetwork.github.io (the "Site") and use our cryptocurrency trading services (the "Services").

By accessing or using our Site and Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Site or Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us, information we collect automatically when you use our Services, and information from other sources.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Creation & Registration:

  • Full legal name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Government-issued identification (passport, driver's license, national ID)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  • Profile picture or avatar
  • Username and password

Financial Information:

  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses
  • Transaction history
  • Payment method information (for fiat on-ramps)
  • Bank account details (if applicable)

Communication:

  • Information you provide when contacting support
  • Feedback and survey responses
  • Social media communications

KYC/AML Verification:

  • Selfie verification photos
  • Additional documentation as required by law
  • Source of funds information (for large transactions)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (type, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring page and exit pages
  • Clickstream data

Transaction Data:

  • Trade history
  • Order book interactions
  • Wallet balances
  • Deposit and withdrawal records
  • API usage (for developers)

Technical Data:

  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Session recordings (with consent)
  • Geolocation data (approximate, based on IP)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Identity verification service providers
  • Blockchain analytics providers
  • Payment processors
  • Law enforcement and regulatory agencies (as required by law)
  • Social media platforms (if you connect your account)
  • Affiliate partners (if you were referred)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • To provide and maintain our Services
  • To process your transactions and trades
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To resolve disputes and provide customer support

3.2 Security & Compliance

  • To detect, prevent, and investigate fraud and security incidents
  • To comply with Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations
  • To monitor for suspicious or illegal activity
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • To respond to law enforcement requests

3.3 Communication

  • To send you transaction confirmations
  • To provide important security notifications
  • To send service updates and announcements
  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To send marketing communications (with your consent, where required)

3.4 Improvement & Analytics

  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our Services
  • To develop new features and functionality
  • To conduct research and analytics
  • To personalize your experience

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

  • When you explicitly authorize us to share your information
  • When you connect to third-party services

4.2 For Legal & Compliance Purposes

  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • To respond to subpoenas, search warrants, or court orders
  • To protect against fraud, abuse, or illegal activity
  • To investigate violations of our Terms of Service
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others

4.3 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our Services:

Identity Verification: Jumio, Onfido, Sumsub (Purpose: KYC/AML compliance | Data: Personal information, government IDs, selfies)

Security & Fraud Prevention: Chainalysis, TRM Labs, Elliptic (Purpose: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity | Data: Transaction data, wallet addresses, IP information)

Infrastructure & Hosting: AWS, Google Cloud, Azure (Purpose: Host and maintain our Services | Data: Technical data, usage information)

Payment Processing: Payment processors, banks (Purpose: Facilitate fiat deposits/withdrawals | Data: Financial information, transaction details)

Analytics: Google Analytics, Mixpanel (Purpose: Understand usage patterns | Data: Anonymized usage data)

4.4 Business Transfers

In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company, we may transfer your information to the acquiring entity.

4.5 Aggregated & Anonymized Data

We may share aggregated, de-identified information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

4.6 Blockchain Transparency

Important: Cryptocurrency transactions are recorded on public blockchains. When you use our Services to conduct transactions, certain information (such as transaction amounts, wallet addresses, and timestamps) is recorded on these public blockchains and may be visible to anyone with access to the blockchain. We have no control over and are not responsible for information made public through blockchain networks.

5. Your Rights & Choices

5.1 Access & Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Request data portability
  • Object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@beastsnetwork.com.

5.2 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by:

  • Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails
  • Contacting us at support@beastsnetwork.com
  • Adjusting your notification preferences in your account settings

Please note that even if you opt out of marketing communications, we may still send you transactional and security-related messages.

5.3 Cookies & Tracking

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. If you prefer, you can usually choose to set your browser to remove or reject browser cookies. Please note that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of our Services.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with our Services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements:

  • Account Information: Retained for as long as your account is active, plus 5 years after account closure for compliance
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • KYC Documents: Retained for 5 years after account closure
  • Communication Records: Retained for 3 years
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt out

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Cold storage for the majority of cryptocurrency assets
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II certification
  • Background checks for employees with data access
  • Data minimization practices
  • Secure deletion procedures

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our primary data processing locations are in:
