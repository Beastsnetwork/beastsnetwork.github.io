New Theoretical Attack on AES-256 Reduces Security Margin

Cryptographers have published a paper describing a new attack on AES-256 that, while not practical against current implementations, reduces the theoretical security margin. The attack requires 2^120 operations, still far beyond current computational capabilities, but highlights the need for post-quantum migration planning.
