What's at Stake

The Pattern

ShinyHunters has demonstrated a year-long pattern of exploiting third-party access points. Federal regulators, including FINRA, have long warned that third-party provider risk is one of the most consistently exploited paths into hardened environments. Verizon's 2026 Data Breach Investigations Report confirms vendor-mediated intrusions as a leading attack pattern.

Action Items

  1. Audit all third-party vendor access immediately
