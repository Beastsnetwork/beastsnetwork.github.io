OnlyFans Mega Leak: 340 Million User Records Exposed

The Breach: Hackers claim to have exposed 340 million user records from OnlyFans, the popular content subscription platform.

Data at Risk:

  • Usernames and email addresses
  • Payment information
  • Content creator and subscriber details
  • Private messages and media

Industry Implications:

  • One of the largest breaches of 2026
  • Highlights the risks of platforms handling sensitive personal and financial data
  • Raises questions about content creator protections

Response:

OnlyFans has not yet confirmed the full scope of the breach. Cybersecurity experts are advising users to:

  1. Change passwords immediately
  2. Monitor financial accounts for unauthorized transactions
  3. Be vigilant for phishing attempts using leaked data
