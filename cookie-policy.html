Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026 | Effective Date: June 28, 2026

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences or login status.

Cookies do not contain any information that personally identifies you, but personal information that we store about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for several purposes on our blog website:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functionality such as page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This allows us to improve our content and user experience.

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your language or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We may use the following types of cookies on our Site:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and expire once you close your browser. They are used to maintain your session on our Site.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period of time specified in the cookie. We use these cookies to identify return visitors to our Site.
  • First-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by our Site directly.
  • Third-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by third-party services that appear on our Site, such as analytics providers.

4. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the Site and to deliver additional functionality. These third-party cookies are set by domains that are not controlled by us.

For example, we use Google Analytics to understand how our visitors use the Site. You can learn more about Google Analytics cookies here.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You have several options when it comes to cookies:

5.1 Browser Settings

If you prefer to avoid the use of cookies on our Site, you may disable cookies in your browser. However, please note that if you do this, you may not be able to access all or parts of our Site. Each browser offers different tools for managing cookies, so please refer to your browser's help materials for more information.

5.2 Opt-Out Tools

You can also use third-party tools to decline cookies. For example, you can opt out of Google Analytics cookies here.

5.3 Do Not Track

Some web browsers offer a "Do Not Track" feature that lets you tell websites that you do not want to have your online activities tracked. Currently, we do not respond to browser "Do Not Track" signals.

6. More Information

If you have any questions about our use of cookies or this Cookie Policy, please contact us at: contact@beastsnetwork.win

For more general information about cookies, please read "All About Cookies".
