Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026 | Effective Date: June 27, 2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how BeastsNetwork ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at https://beastsnetwork.github.io (the "Site") and use our cryptocurrency trading services (the "Services").

By using our Site and Services, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions.

We also use similar technologies such as:

  • Web beacons: Small graphic images that help us understand how users interact with our Site
  • Pixel tags: Small blocks of code that allow us to track user activity
  • Local storage: Data stored directly on your device
  • Session storage: Data stored temporarily during your browsing session

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our Site:

Category Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary These cookies are essential for the Site to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submission. Without these cookies, the Site cannot function properly. Session or persistent
Performance These cookies help us understand how you interact with our Site by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They allow us to count visits and traffic sources to measure and improve the performance of our Site. Persistent (up to 2 years)
Functional These cookies allow the Site to remember choices you make (such as your language preference) and provide enhanced, more personal features. They may also be used to provide services you have asked for, such as watching a video. Persistent (up to 2 years)
Targeting/Advertising These cookies are used to make advertising messages more relevant to you and your interests. They may be used to deliver targeted advertising or to limit the number of times you see an advertisement. Persistent (up to 2 years)
Security These cookies help identify and prevent security risks. They may be used to detect fraudulent activity or to protect user data from unauthorized access. Session or persistent

4. Cookies We Use

Below is a list of the specific cookies we use on our Site:

4.1 First-Party Cookies

These are cookies set by our Site directly:

  • Session ID: Used to maintain your session state. Strictly Necessary
  • Authentication Token: Used to verify your logged-in status. Strictly Necessary Security
  • Preference Settings: Remembers your display preferences (e.g., dark mode). Functional
  • CSRF Token: Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks. Security

4.2 Third-Party Cookies

These are cookies set by third-party services we use:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us analyze how users interact with our Site. Performance
  • Google Fonts: Used to display fonts on our Site. Functional
  • Hotjar (if enabled): Provides heatmaps and session recordings to help us improve user experience. Performance

5. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and maintain your logged-in session
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity and protect your account
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our Site and identify areas for improvement
  • Performance: To monitor Site performance and ensure it functions properly
  • Marketing: To deliver relevant content and advertisements (with your consent where required)

6. Your Cookie Choices

You have several options to manage cookies on our Site:

6.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies you have and delete them on an individual basis
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from particular websites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that if you block or delete cookies, some features of our Site may not function properly.

6.2 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our Site, you may see a cookie consent banner. This banner allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

6.3 Opt-Out Options

For third-party cookies, you can also opt out through the following services:

  • Google Analytics: You can opt out of Google Analytics cookies by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Digital Advertising Alliance: You can opt out of interest-based advertising by visiting aboutads.info.
  • Network Advertising Initiative: You can opt out of targeted advertising by visiting networkadvertising.org.

7. Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session cookies: Expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them
  • First-party cookies: Typically expire within 1-2 years
  • Third-party cookies: Retention periods are determined by the third party

8. International Data Transfers

Information collected through cookies may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from those in your country.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site and Services are not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information through cookies from children under 18.
